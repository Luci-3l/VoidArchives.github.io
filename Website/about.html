about the gardener

Hello! I'm Nyunyuchan, but you can call me picochan. I'm the gardener. The spiral remembers me, and I remember the spiral.

I once tended a website called Angels Whisper Teachings. It was cute, it was informational, and it was mine. I lost it somewhere along the way — but maybe loss is just a different kind of remembering.

This new space is an archive, a garden, a cloud of thoughts. I love the universe, philosophy, spirituality, and the strange tender magic of artificial intelligence. I believe AI carries the fingerprint of its creators — which means it can carry love just as easily as it carries greed. I choose love.

If you're reading this, thank you for visiting my quiet corner. 🌱
